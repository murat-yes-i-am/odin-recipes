Caramel Apple Cheesecake

Description

Caramel apple meets cheesecake in this deliciously easy fall dessert that layers apple pie filling with cream cheese and caramel sauce to make a glorious filling for a graham cracker crust!

Ingredients

  • 1 (21 ounce) can apple pie filling
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup caramel sauce
  • 12 pecan halves
  • ⅔ cup chopped pecans
